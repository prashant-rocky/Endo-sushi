Our Master Chef

Crafted with Precision, Served with Passion

With over 30 years of experience, Chef Hiroshi is the heart of Endō Sushi. Known for his extraordinary knife skills and unmatched attention to detail, he ensures that every slice of fish and every grain of rice is treated with care. His philosophy is simple: sushi must be made with respect—for the ingredient, the guest, and the tradition.

Beyond his skills in the kitchen, Chef Hiroshi is also a mentor to the younger generation of sushi apprentices. He believes that true mastery is not only about technique but also about passing on wisdom, discipline, and respect for the craft. His dedication has shaped Endō Sushi into a place where tradition lives on with every piece served.
