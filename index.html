Our Story

Crafting Sushi, Sharing Smiles

At Endō Sushi, our journey began in Osaka’s bustling Central Fish Market, where freshness and quality have always been the heart of every dish. For generations, we have carried forward the art of Edomae-style sushi, carefully selecting the best seasonal seafood each morning to ensure every bite reflects the essence of traditional Japanese flavor.

But sushi for us is more than just food—it is a way to share hospitality, warmth, and culture with every guest. From the early hours of dawn until midday, our chefs craft sushi that blends time-honored techniques with an atmosphere that feels both welcoming and authentic.
